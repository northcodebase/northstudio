Studio FAQ

Why build another software studio?

Much of modern software is bloated, loaded with invasive telemetry, and over-engineered. NorthStudio stands for quiet utilities: applications that do one thing, do it exceptionally well, and respect your resources.

Are the projects open source?

Yes. Standard software projects are open source under community licenses (MIT, GPL, etc.). I believe building in public creates better software architecture and developer integrity.

How are these projects funded?

NorthStudio is self-funded, independent, and student-run. This allows complete freedom to prioritize code quality, simplicity, and longevity over commercial monetization constraints.
